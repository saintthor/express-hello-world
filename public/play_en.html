For ACC, there are many private blockchains that can be transfered from one to another. Each blockchain is an NFT. Setting different object to each blockchain to build a business.

First, create players.
Then, create blockchains, each for one object.

Now you see the players and blockchains in the pannel. There is only one block in each blockchain.

let us make[{{ SelUser?SelUser.Name:'some sender' }}] add a new block on [{{ SelChain?SelChain.Name:'some blockchain' }}] after [{{ SelBlockId?SelBlockId:'some block' }}] to transfer the blockchain to [{{ SelTarget?SelTarget.Name:'some receiver' }}].

Follow the below steps to select the four "some" variables in the square above.

1. Click a player on the pannel to select the sender.

2. Click a blockchain to be transfered on the pannel. You can see the block list on the blockchain after clicking.

3. Click a block as the target block in the block list , which the new block will follow.

4. Click a player on the pannel again to select the receiver.

For legal transfers：
1. The target block must be the last on the blockchain.
2. The sender must be the receiver of the transfer written in the target block. That means the sender owns the blockchain.
Otherwise, the transfer may be ignored, the sender may be belittled, or even blocked.。
